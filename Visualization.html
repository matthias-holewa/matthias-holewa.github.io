Tegelsbarg Park
HHVH Landschaftsarchitekten GmbH
This park is part of an urban development program called RISE in Hamburg, Germany aiming at the renewal of existing infrastructure. This project won the official submission in 2021 and currently is in avtive development. My work shows 1/3 of the planned restructuring including an enlarged playground area and new places for sporting activities. I created this environment based on the technical plan.
